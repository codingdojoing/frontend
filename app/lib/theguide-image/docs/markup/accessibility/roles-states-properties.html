In case of decorative use

When the image is used for decorative purposes only, do not add ARIA properties. Unnecessary use of ARIA roles, states and properties will have a negative impact on accessibility.

In that case make the alt text empty (alt=""). This indicates to assistive technologies that the image can be safely ignored.
